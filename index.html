Milk spilling Control Unit

Wireless Milk Boil Control Unit helps to control the boiling of milk wirelessly. It saves time and also prevents loss of milk. It is not confined to just indicating or alarming but also controlling the boiling process. Whether on gas or on a hot plate, stay safe every time. This machine is self-sustainable and need not be charged, it charges itself using the waste heat around it.

View Project

Continuous Variable Generator

CV Gen system is a retrofitting unit that can be fitted in between the engine and alternator of any type of Genset without making any change in the engine and alternator and helps in optimization of fuel consumption according to the load conditions without hampering other parameters of electricity. The size and capacity of the CVG unit depend on the capacity of the Genset. This invention is a smart way to save fuel and optimize power generation.

View Project

Automatic Solar Panel Cleaner

This device is made according to Indian context, as the profile of dust and dirt deposition upon the solar panels are quite different as compared with the countries from where other solar panel cleaning device are imported from that is the reason imported automatic machines are not capable to clean the solar panels in India efficiently.It is a fully automated device which is to be used in cleaning solar panels efficiently.It can clean dust, dirt and droppings of birds from the Solar Panels.

View Project

E-Trike

Smart Trike is a E-Vehicle having tadpole type structure. It is smart because it is having various sensors in it like centrifugal sensor, Running stabilizer, Pneumatic tilt control, etc. These things take care of the comfort and safety of the passenger as well as driver.
