Our Projects

We at NewGen IEDC ITS Engineering College try to make our imaginations come to reality. Some of our Projects and Patents are listed below.




  • Acceleration Enhancer

    Acceleration Enhancer helps in enhancing acceleration while maintaining optimum mileage in Electric Vehicals.

  • Cutting Fluid Optimiser

    Cutting Fluid Optimise helps in reducing the wastage of cutting fluid while giving better life to the cutting tool and giving the surface finish as per need.

  • Milk Spilling Control Unit

    Wireless Milk Boil Control Unit helps to control the boiling of milk wirelessly. It saves time and also prevents loss of milk.

  • Smart Sense whitewash Technology

    Instead of using rollers or brushes this technology uses sprays which saves time and prevents spilling of paint all over floor.

  • Smart Down Rod

    Smart Down Rod is a solution for Easy Cleaning/repairing of fans and Preventing Suicide by Hanging from Ceiling Fans.

  • Self Cleaning Dining Table

    Self Cleaning Dining Table cleans itself automatically and also eliminates coronavirus using the UVC light present in it.

  • 3D Solar

    3D Solar is a smarter and cheaper way of generating solar energy. It focuses sunlight from all directions and produces more energy as compared to a grid of solar cells.

  • Continuous Variable Generator

    It helps in optimization of fuel consumption according to the load conditions without hampering other parameters of electricity.

  • E-Sugarcane Crusher

    In E-Sugarcne Crusher, a diesel engine is substituted by an electric DC motor which is run by lithium ferro phosphate battery to extract the sugarcane juice.

  • E-Trike

    It is a vehicle having tadpole type structure, with various sensors in it like centrifugal sensor, Running stabilizer, Pneumatic tilt control, etc. It ensures the comfort and safety of the driver.

  • EV-VCU

    EV-VCU is an integrated system with capabilities to accommodate any and all EV components. It is a Made in India Product which can be used in any Electric Vehicle.

  • Rail Generator

    The machine discloses electrical generators for power generation, wherein all rotors either all same or combination of different types rotate in opposite direction.

  • Smart Card Glue Inspection Machine

    This Glue Inspection System is a system based on Machine Vision and Artificial Intelligence. This system is used to inspect the Glue present on chips of E-cards.

  • Zero Energy Toilet

    This machine is made for rural areas of developing countries, the choices for pumping water are often limited because reliable or affordable sources of power are not available.

  • Wireless Laptop Charging Platform

    Wireless laptop charging platform is a device which can charge upto 4 laptops and illustrate how magnetic coupling can be used to transfer energy wirelessly.

  • Oxygen Monitoring Multi-Functional Convertible Robot

    This robot can monitor and supply oxygen using the oxygen concentrator present in it and can also be converted into a wheelchair.

  • Traffic Data Extractor

    It is a technology that helps in collecting useful traffic data catering to heterogeneous as well as homogeneous traffic conditions of the developing world.

  • E-Smart Bike

    E-Smart Bike is an electric bike with various smart features like fingerprint ON/OFF system, SOS Emergency feature, smart stand , etc.

  • Automatic Solar Panel Cleaner

    It is a fully automated device which is to be used in cleaning solar panels efficiently.It can clean dust, dirt and droppings of birds from the Solar Panels.

  • Automatic Shoe Polish Machine

    This machine can clean and polish shoes with no need to touch the shoes and our hands remain clean, as the shoe polish also contains toxic fumes and chemicals .

  • Recption Robot

    It is robot which is able to meet and greet visitors or clients and Inform them about your college or company it can also tell news or products information, display video, etc.

  • Sewage Cleaning Machine

    Sewage Cleaning Machine is used to clean the sewer which was previously done my workers entering the manholes themselves.

  • Smart Gardening System

    It is an IOT based automatic gardening system which can water plants by sensing the moisture content of the Plant’s soil.
