Our Programs

NewGen IEDC is the programme launched by NSTEDB, DST, Government of India and implemented by EDII, Ahmedabad. Several Programs and Events are organized during the time, few of these are listed below.


  • FDP

    The Faculty Development Programme intends to provide financial assistance to facilitate up-gradation of knowledge, skill and intends to provide opportunities for induction training to teachers.

  • WEDP

    Women Entrepreneurship Development Programme focuses to train S&T graduates and diploma holders to build the capacity of Women-Owned Businesses across India.

  • EDP

    Entrepreneurship Development Program aims at training the graduates in the essentials of conceiving, planning, initiating and launching an economic activity successfully.

  • TEDP

    Technology Entrepreneurship Development Program focuses on training and development need of Science and Technology entrepreneurs in a specific technology areas.
